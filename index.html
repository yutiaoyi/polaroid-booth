Privacy Policy

Photos are processed locally in your browser. This open-source photo booth does not upload, sell, or share your images, and no account is required to use it.

FAQ

Do my photos leave my device?

No. Camera frames, uploaded images, and generated strips stay in your browser unless you choose to download or share them yourself.

Can I use this project for an event or personal page?

Yes. You can use it as inspiration or adapt it for personal, community, and open-source projects. Please keep proper attribution when you reuse the work.

Why does the camera ask for permission?

The browser requires permission before any webpage can access your camera. The app only uses the camera after you choose to start taking photos.
